SEC-1: Broken Access Control (OWASP A01)

IDCheckResultEvidence
SEC-1.1 BU_HEAD cannot access data outside their managed Business Unit PASS assertCanManage() in leave-requests.service.ts verifies leave.user.department.businessUnitId === managedBuId and throws 403 for cross-BU access. findAll() applies the same BU filter at the query level so data is never returned to BU_HEAD for other BUs.
SEC-1.2 BU_HEAD cannot access admin-only endpoints PASS Business Units, Departments, Clients, Org Structure, and Activity endpoints are protected by @Roles('SUPER_USER', 'ADMIN') or similar guards. BU_HEAD is not listed. RolesGuard is globally active and rejects non-matching roles.
SEC-1.3 BU_HEAD cannot promote themselves or change their managed BU PASS The profile PATCH endpoint only allows updating name, phone, DOB, profile photo. System role and managed BU changes require SUPER_USER/ADMIN privileges via the users management endpoint.
